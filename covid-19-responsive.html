COVID-19 RESPONSE & SAFETY INFORMATION

A Message From Our President

Amid the ongoing uncertainty surrounding the coronavirus, we remain open and committed to serving the Boulder community and meeting your needs as best we can. In an effort to ensure the safety and well-being of you – our customers – as well as our employees, we will be implementing a number of precautionary changes until further notice. Please continue to check back here for updates moving forward.


UPDATE: Regular Store Hours Change

Effective May 24, 2021 Until Further Notice

Monday – Saturday: 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM

Sunday: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM


Beginning Monday, May 24 we will be extending our Saturday store hours, opening one hour earlier for all customers at 8AM and closing one hour later than previously at 7 PM. Sunday hours will remain 9AM - 6PM. Curbside Pickup service will also continue to be available everyday during store hours.


Curbside Pickup orders can be claimed outside the North side of the store. Please use designated curbside pickup parking spots and call the number posted to let us know when you have arrived.


UPDATE: Masks No Longer Required To Shop In-Store

Effective May 17, 2021 Until Further Notice


Beginning Monday, May 17, in accordance with Boulder County guidelines, we will no longer be requiring our customers to wear face coverings inside the store. Masks are still highly encouraged, especially for anyone less than two weeks removed from receiving their second vaccination, but will not be enforced. The same policy applies for our employees.

What McGuckin Hardware Is Doing For Your Safety

  • We have added a curbside pickup option to our online store and are accepting phone orders.
  • We are sanitizing frequent touch areas around the store.
  • We are performing store cleanings more frequently.
  • We are requiring any McGuckin employee who is feeling unwell to stay home.
  • We are adding items to our online store every day to allow customers to shop from home.
  • We have postponed or cancelled in-store events until further notice.
  • We are following the guidelines from the CDC and government agencies.
  • We will continue to closely monitor the situation and adjust as needed.


Please feel free to contact us if you have questions or concerns about our policies. We appreciate your patience, cooperation and continued support during such trying circumstances. We look forward to continuing to serve you in these and better days ahead! As always – thanks for shopping with McGuckin!

Sincerely and with well wishes,

Barry Hight – President, McGuckin Hardware
