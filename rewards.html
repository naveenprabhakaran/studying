Frequently Asked Questions About Our Program


How do I sign up?

It’s easy. Just go online to  mcguckin.com/register, take just a few minutes to register and the next time you come in you can have your first $5 coupon ready to go! Of course, you can register at check out as well, but why wait!

I was an ACE Rewards member at McGuckin Hardware—am I already enrolled in McGuckin Rewards?

Almost. You will just need to re-register your account online using the same email address you used for ACE Rewards. Just go to  mcguckin.com/register. After validation, you should then be able to view your past purchases and find your first $5 coupon in your Rewards Dashboard.

How do I set up my new account?

New accounts are done exactly the same way. Just click My Account on the McGuckin website, hit  Register and follow the prompts to begin receiving your points as well as so many other benefits.

Who can join?

All McGuckin Hardware customers are encouraged to join. The only requirement is that you are 18 years old or older and have a valid email address. Individuals only. McGuckin employees are not eligible.

What can I expect out of my McGuckin Rewards account?

In a word… Control. Your new McGuckin Rewards Dashboard provides you with an unprecedented level of control over your shopping experience with us. With your Dashboard you can:

  • See your current point level and how close you are to your next Rewards coupon.
  • Every time you earn a new Rewards coupon, it will come through your Dashboard. No more lost coupons, just print your reward or have it scanned from your phone. Never miss out again.
  • You never have to worry about getting your Rewards points, whether you purchase in store or online, they are automatically applied to your new McGuckin Rewards account.
  • Keep an accurate account of your past purchases and have ready access to purchase information.
  • Keep an accurate record of your receipts! No longer will you need to worry about where you left your paper receipt, you will always have a digital copy as near as your cell phone or home computer.
  • You can choose to have a copy of your receipt emailed to you or sent via text to your phone. (As well as having them stored on your McGuckin Rewards Dashboard.)
  • No more cards to carry or worry about losing.
  • You will receive emails and updates about special savings (which you can opt out of if you so choose).

How do I earn points?

Nothing could be easier or more fun… just go shopping! You’ll earn 5 points for every dollar that you spend with us. When you’ve reached 1250 points, you will automatically receive a $5 coupon to spend on in-store purchases. We will also send you an email or text (depending upon the preferences you set) to inform you that you’ve earned a new Reward. This way you’ll never miss them.

For customers who earn more than 1000 points in a single transaction, we have made it easier than ever to earn your rewards. The award levels for points earned in a single transaction are as follows:

  • 1,250 points = $5 coupon
  • 2,500 points = $10 coupon
  • 3,750 points = $15 coupon
  • 5,000 points = $20 coupon
  • 6,250 points = $25 coupon
  • 7,500 points = $30 coupon
  • 8,750 points = $35 coupon
  • 10,000 points = $40 coupon
  • 11,250 points = $45 coupon
  • 12,500 points = $50 coupon

Please note: any points earned above 12,500 will be counted toward reward coupons. No points will be lost. Any single transaction over 15,000 points will receive additional an additional $10 coupon. Any points that do not reach the above thresholds will be saved and count toward your next $5 Reward.

What purchases are eligible for points?

With the possible exception of some “Special” sales, every purchase earns points. In fact, look closely at our circular and you will often see extra savings attached to some purchases thanks to your Rewards membership. Not to mention special “Double Rewards Points” sales.

Are online purchases eligible for points?

Absolutely! It doesn’t matter if you purchase in store or online, you’ll get rewards points either way. Also, you can use your coupons for purchases made online or in store.

Can I use my rewards coupons for online purchases?

YES YOU CAN! You can earn points & use Rewards coupons for online AND in-store purchases! It’s easier than ever to use your exclusive savings. Come in or log in and experience your Rewards today.

What happens to my points if I return an item?

Of course, we always hope that you’ll be satisfied with any purchase you might make at McGuckin. And as always, there are no returns on weather related and clearance items; but should you need to return an item occasionally, as a courtesy, we will not remove your points.

What do I do if I’m missing points I should’ve received?

Because of the nature of the systems involved, please allow at least 24 to 48 hours from the time of sale for points to register on your Dashboard. If your points are still not showing after that time, please contact the store closest to you or in which you made your purchase.

Do my points or coupons have an expiration date?

Your points do not have an expiration date, however coupons do expire depending on the type of coupon:

  • Signup coupon: 90 day expiration from date issued
  • Rewards coupons: 30 day expiration from date issued
  • Birthday coupon: Expires at the end of your birthday month
