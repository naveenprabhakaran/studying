If you're interested in joining the team of Green Vests, you are welcome to apply for employment online or stop by our reception desk located at the east end of the store to fill out an application.

THE McGUCKIN WAY


Customer satisfaction and retention are the lifeblood of our organization. Our vision statement is our goal and our core values are how we’ll get there. It is our expectation that all employees live by these concepts and exhibit them in their daily work.


Vision Statement:


It's pretty simple. McGuckin Hardware thrives on providing an unparalleled customer experience. To accomplish this goal we rely on our core values.


Core Values:


Embody Patience − We are dedicated to respecting our
customers.


Understand The Ask − We listen to our customers to truly
understand their needs.


Facilitate Discovery − We work as a team and leverage
our combined knowledge to solve our customers’ problems.
